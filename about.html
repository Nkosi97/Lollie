About

Amkele Ngongomana was born on September 19,1998, in the Eastern Cape. later moved to Cape town to start pre-school. Started high school in 2012 at Bellville south high school matriculated in 2016 Did the furthest studies at Damelin College at Mowbray Campus studying Journalism and media studies, will soon be graduating in the course on April 2020. Did an internship for one of the community Radio station (RadioKhaltsha) for 5 months. Had her programme called HipHop Drive alongside with Ayabonga Madlebe, that gave her a great platform to interview local artists and promote their music through their show, even had the opportunity to interview lolli dekeni the champion of Emtee #RAP4ME talent in 2019 discovered the love for radio and 2020 started a Radio production course at Radio zibonele fm . Amkele is the founder and owner of LolliePhotography, she always had a passion for photography from a young age that’s when she decided to pursue a business in photography, worked with young talented girls who are pursuing a career in modeling helping them create a profile.

Mission,Vision Goal


The mission is to provide an enjoyable experience in front of the camera as well as timeless photographs that you will treasure for a lifetime my goals is to capture images in a form of a story. The vision is ‘’ to be with you for all of life’s important moments ‘’
